Overview

Amp Pay is a custodial onchain banking product operating on Multi-chain. It offers a Visa card with Up to 4% cashback paid in USD.

Key features include Up to 4% cash back, Up to 12% APY savings, No credit check, Black Card tier, Free P2P payments, Crypto spending. The card has no annual fee, making it accessible to all users.

Amp Pay is available globally, supporting Visa payments at millions of merchants worldwide.
