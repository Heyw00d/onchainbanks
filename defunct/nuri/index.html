What Happened

Nuri (formerly Bitwala) was a German fintech that offered full IBAN bank accounts with integrated Bitcoin and Ethereum trading. The service included a Visa debit card.

The company first faced challenges in January 2018 when WaveCrest lost its Visa license, forcing them to pause card operations. They recovered by partnering with Solarisbank.

In 2022, the crypto market downturn hit hard. Nuri cited the "tough economical & political environment" and couldn't secure new funding or find an acquirer.

German banking regulations provided some protection: fiat funds in the Solarisbank accounts were safe. However, crypto holdings needed to be transferred out before the shutdown.
